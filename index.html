The Minds Behind the Code

We are Manada and Sinan, a collaborative coding duo and students at Vidya Academy of Science and Technology. Our partnership has led to the successful completion of over 25 projects, a journey that has honed our skills in creating robust and user-friendly digital solutions.

Our dedication to innovation and excellence was recognized with the prestigious National Coders Award, presented to us by Narendra Modi himself. This achievement fuels our passion for pushing the boundaries of technology and solving complex challenges.

We specialize in full-stack development, with a talent for transforming ideas into functional, elegant web applications. From front-end design to back-end architecture, we work seamlessly to deliver high-quality results. Our key projects include developing the "Saved" feature for Instagram and the "Blend" feature for a popular platform.
